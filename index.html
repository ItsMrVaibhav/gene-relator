Gene Relator

Gene Relator is a software capable of comparing gene sequences of organisms such as humans, apes, fishes, etc. and computing several essencial results about the organisms. The results computed can further be used to infer similarity between organisms and understand their evolutionary relationships.

About

Gene Relator is a software dedicated to compare gene sequences of various organisms, compute essencial results, and find similarity between the organisms.

// Information about Gene Sequences and their comparison goes here...

Currently, the databse of this software has gene sequences of 10 organisms. More sequences can be added anytime in order to scale the software.

Following are the organisms whose gene sequences are currently available in the database.

  • Human
  • Gorilla
  • Danio Rerio (Zebrafish)
  • Canis Lupus (Wolf)
  • Macaca Mulatta (Rhesus Monkey)
  • Bos Taurus (Cattle)
  • Gallus (Chicken)
  • Pan Troglodytes (Chimpanzee)
  • Caenorhabditis Elegans (Roundworm)
  • Sus Scrofa (Wild Boar)
